Full Stack Of Pancakes

In February of 2010, I began working for General Mills. I started working as a low wage laborer but over the course of 4 years I worked my way into a position as a Technical Operator. Working on a team of 20 people, I thought that this is where I would be for the rest of my life. By July of 2016, General Mills announced it would close our factory. I realized I needed a change in my life. I knew I love computer software, so I began a journey of self studying, picking up books on programming in C++, Javascript, HTML/CSS, and Python. But, my learning was slow going. So when I heard of Galvanize, I thought it might be the chance I need to change my life. Now, in 2017, I can confidently call myself a web developer.
